Additional Considerations

  • We could choose to publish event pages for some events and not others.
  • We might also publish event pages after the event has passed and once there is media to display.
  • What other event-specific details could affect the presentation of the event? What do we display for a virtual event?
  • We will also need to plan for updating the contents of a page pre- and post-event. For example, the general description will need updating from what will be awesome to what was awesome (with specific details).
